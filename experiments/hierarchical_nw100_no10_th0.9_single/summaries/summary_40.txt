The Higher Education Act of 1965 (HEA) is being considered for reauthorization. Title IV of theact authorizes the major federal student aid programs. In FY2003, the federal student loan programs provided $45.8 billion in new loans to students and their parents. The FFEL and DL programs provide the following types of loans: Stafford loans (subsidized and unsubsidized): Lowinterest, variable rate loans available to undergraduate and graduate students. Consolidation loans: Loans that provide borrowersrefinancing options.
