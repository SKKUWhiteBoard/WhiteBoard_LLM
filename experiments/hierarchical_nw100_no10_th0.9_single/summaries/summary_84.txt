Both the House and the Senate are considering legislation to support medical innovation. Reforms to the National Institutes of Health (NIH) and changes to the drug, biologic and device approval pathways at the Food and Drug Administration (FDA) Both NIH and FDA are agencies within the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) The House passed H.R. 6 , the 21 st Century Cures Act, on July 10, 2015, on a vote of 344 to 77. The Senate's medical innovation package is that chamber's companion effort to the House's 21 st century Cures initiative.
