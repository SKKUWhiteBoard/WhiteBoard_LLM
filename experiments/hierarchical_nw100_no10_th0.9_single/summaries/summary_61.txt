Title V of the Social Security Act (SSA) authorizes funding for services and projects. Title V was enacted by Congress in 1935 to promote and support the health of mothers and children. The MCH Services Block Grant was appropriated $638.2 million in funding for FY2016. The majority of this funding is distributed to the 50 states, the District of Columbia, Puerto Rico, Guam, the Virgin Islands, American Samoa, the Federated States of Micronesia, and Palau.
