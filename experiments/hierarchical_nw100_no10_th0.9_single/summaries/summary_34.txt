The panelists attributed many of the ATC modernization program’s chronic problems to cultural and technical factors. They cited resistance to change at all levels within the agency and insufficient technical expertise as key factors impeding modernization. The panel was not representative of all potential stakeholders, but it provided a rich dialogue, the authors say. They say the panel provided strong messages in responding to each of the panelists’ questions and comments. The authors say more thought, discussion, and research are needed to develop greater agreement on what we really know.
