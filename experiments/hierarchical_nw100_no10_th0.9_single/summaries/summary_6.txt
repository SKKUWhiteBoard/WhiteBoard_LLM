Military construction appropriations fund the planning, design, construction, alteration, and improvement of military facilities worldwide. As of fiscal year 2006, DOD had 3,731 installations, with 766 installations located overseas. DOD has been reexamining overseas basing requirements to allow for greater U.S. military flexibility to combat conventional and asymmetric threats worldwide. Guam provides strategic flexibility, freedom of action, and prompt global action for the Global War on Terrorism, peace and crisis response, DOD says.
