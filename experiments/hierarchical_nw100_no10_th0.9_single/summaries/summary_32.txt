Security experts have raised concerns that terrorists may try to smuggle radiological or nuclear materials into the U.S. No terrorists have detonated a dirty bomb in a city, but Chechen separatists placed a canister containing cesium-137 in a Moscow park in the mid- 1990s. FEMA has not completed planning to help cities and states recover from RDD or IND incidents, says Peter Bergen. Bergen: FEMA has placed a higher priority on developing a strategy to respond to domestic RDD and IND incidents.
