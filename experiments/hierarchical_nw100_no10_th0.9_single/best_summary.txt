From October 1, 2010, until the enactment of this legislation on April 11, 2011, the legislative branch operated on continuing resolutions. Division G of the FY2012 Consolidated Appropriations Act ( H.R. 2055 , P.L. 112-74 ) provided $4.307 billion in discretionary funding. This level is $236.9 million (5.2%) below the FY2011 enacted level. The legislative branch represents approximately 0.4% of the total discretionary appropriations provided in this act.
