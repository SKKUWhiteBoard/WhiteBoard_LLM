Michael Short talks about radiation damage and nuclear materials. He explains the different material properties and what they actually mean. Short will be giving a talk at MRS, a materials research conference tomorrow. The talk will be on radiation damage in nuclear materials, which is the biggest research field in MIT's physics department. The course is free and available to the public.
