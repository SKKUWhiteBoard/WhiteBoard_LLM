In January 2003, we designated implementation and transformation of the new Department of Homeland Security as high risk. One of the department’s key challenges is integrating the components’ respective financial management systems, many of which were outdated and had limited functionality, as well as addressing weaknesses from the inherited components. Failure to effectively carry out its mission would potentially expose the nation to very serious consequences, the report says. The report says that without sound internal control and accurate and timely financial information, it is not possible to accomplish the agenda and secure the best performance.
