Obesity rates have risen substantially in recent decades, from 13% of the adult population in the 1960s to 36% in 2009-2010. The number of calories consumed has increased from a daily average of 1,875 calories during 1977-1978 to 2,067 calories during 2005-2008. Calorie labeling on menus has been adopted as one approach to making consumers aware of the amount of calories they are consuming. The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention have deemed menu labeling a "winnable battle"
