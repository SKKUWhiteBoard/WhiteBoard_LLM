Social media can be accessed by computers, tablets, smart and cellular telephones, and mobile telephone text messaging (SMS) The use of social media is an evolving phenomenon. Rapid changes in communication technologies in the past decade have enabled people to interact and share information through media that were non-existent or widely unavailable as recently as 15 years ago. In the last 10 years social media has played an increasing role in emergencies and disasters in both the public and private sectors. For example, during Hurricane Gustav, a Community Emergency Response Team used social media to send mass email notifications to team members through Facebook.
